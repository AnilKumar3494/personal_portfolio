My Projects

Bus Reservation System

In today's bus transportation industry, many private bus transportation companies use the old method of handling work on paper, which seems to be costly and time-consuming. It is also not assured that all transactions will be error-free, which may result in many computation errors in bill production and pricing. The solution to this challenge is to develop an online bus ticket booking system. Customers may purchase tickets online and the bus ticket cannot be lost, stolen, or left behind. Furthermore, the online system allows clients to verify the availability of bus tickets before purchasing them and clients do not need to pay cash for bus tickets since they may pay for them while going.


Programming Languages Used: Springboot, Angular13, CSS3, HTML5, JSP, JPA.
